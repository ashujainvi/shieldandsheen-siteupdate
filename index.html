About Us

Our team is Xpel factory trained. The installation performed on your vehicle is parallel to Xpel's standards.

Background

I have been passionate about cars as fas as I can remember so learning how to properly care for them followed closely. Over the years I have come across numerous car care challenges and learnt to tackle them duly. Through trial and error I have honed my skills and acquired proper tools to detail resultantly.

Founded in 2014, I set out to share my passion and knowledge by opening mobile detailing and an online store for detailing products and tools. I wanted to add paint protection film so I got XPEL factory training in San Antonio, Texas. Also I am a member of the International Detailing Association (IDA) through which I attend various web seminars to constantly keep my self up to date with the latest techniques and tools. Recently attended “Advanced Wet Sanding Techniques” training hosted by Rupes polishers.

New Location

Recently acquired a location at 1392 US 130 South Robbinsville, NJ 08561 to provide one stop shop for premium car care products & tools from Adam’s Polishes, Sonax, MetroVac, Grit Guard, CarPro, ScanGrip and more boutique brands. Also to provide services such as paint protection/correction and XPEL Ultimate Self-Healing paint protection film installation. Shield & Sheen is a CCTV and security alarm monitored shop.

What We Offer

We’re committed to protecting your vehicle from every day road hazards so you may drive a new vehicle everyday.

Before installation, we will remove above surface contaminants using claying method. This will ensure that the film adheres to the vehicle properly. We inspect the work during the installation step so as to fix the issue right away. Before delivery a final inspection is conducted and lastly vehicle is cleaned.
